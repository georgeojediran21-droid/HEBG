Nutrition Tips for Vegetarian Living
  • Protein: Combine legumes (beans, lentils) with grains for complete proteins
  • Iron: Eat iron-rich foods like spinach, lentils, and fortified cereals with vitamin C
  • B12: Ensure adequate intake through fortified foods or supplements
  • Variety: Eat a rainbow of vegetables to maximize micronutrient intake
  • Healthy Fats: Include avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil daily
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water and herbal teas throughout the day
